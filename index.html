Case Study · 2024–2025

VOLL Flights

Reducing offline support and usability friction

Role
Product Designer
Timeline
2 months
Platform
Web & Mobile (Flutter)
Company
VOLL
Context

Corporate travel management platform

VOLL offers Car Rentals, Urban Mobility, Accommodation, and Air Tickets to Brazilian companies. After two years since launch, user feedback and support logs revealed critical usability gaps — problems directly costing the business in offline support volume.

A VOLL oferece Aluguel de Carros, Mobilidade Urbana, Hospedagem e Passagens Aéreas para empresas brasileiras. Após dois anos do lançamento, feedbacks e logs de suporte revelaram lacunas críticas de usabilidade — problemas que custavam diretamente ao negócio em volume de suporte offline.

The problem

At least 15% of users called support instead of booking online

The experience generated enough confusion that users abandoned the platform mid-flow and resorted to phone support. Three core issues drove this:

A experiência gerava confusão suficiente para os usuários abandonarem a plataforma no meio do fluxo. Três problemas centrais causavam isso:

No total price visibility Users reached the end of the booking flow without ever seeing the full round-trip cost. Os usuários chegavam ao fim do fluxo sem nunca ver o custo total da viagem de ida e volta.
Three-tab navigation Outbound, Return, and Combined fares split across separate tabs. 20% didn't know what "Combined" meant. Tarifas de Ida, Volta e Combinadas em abas separadas. 20% não sabiam o que significava "Combinado".
Visual and language overload Excessive colors and technical terminology that didn't match how travelers naturally speak about flights. Cores excessivas e terminologia técnica que não correspondiam à forma natural de falar sobre voos.
Discovery

The previous experience — annotated

Solution

5 decisions that removed friction from end to end

Simpler search headerReduced input fields and visual noise.
Unified date + period calendarOne component replacing four separate inputs.
Loading status barProgress indicator during the 60-second search — replacing a blank screen.
Round-trip price from the first resultFull trip cost on every outbound card. No surprises at checkout.
Inline return selectionBest returns appear below the selected outbound — one page, one flow.
Testing

Moderated sessions + async Maze testing

5 moderated sessions via Google Meet with recorded video, plus async testing via Maze. Key finding 1: Date calendar with time-period sliders caused friction — simplified in final version. Key finding 2: Showing return flights directly below outbound confused 4 of 5 participants — redesigned as a dedicated step.

5 sessões moderadas via Google Meet com vídeo gravado, mais testes assíncronos via Maze. Achado 1: Calendário com sliders de período causava atrito — simplificado na versão final. Achado 2: Mostrar retorno abaixo da ida confundiu 4 de 5 participantes — redesenhado como etapa dedicada.

Case Study · 2021–2024

Doris Widget

Increasing feature adoption for a virtual try-on tool

Role
Lead Product Designer
Timeline
3 years
Platform
Mobile · E-commerce
Company
Doris
Context

Virtual try-on embedded in Brazilian e-commerce

Doris allows customers to virtually try on clothes and receive precise sizing recommendations through a widget embedded in partner stores. The flow required 2 full-body photos. The product was technically sound. The adoption wasn't.

A Doris permite que clientes provem roupas virtualmente e recebam recomendacoes precisas de tamanho por um widget integrado a lojas parceiras. O fluxo exigia 2 fotos de corpo inteiro. O produto era tecnicamente solido. A adocao, nao.

The problem

Only 1.2% of activated users completed the task

User tests in controlled environments couldn't predict that real-world context — time, place, privacy, lighting — would be a significant barrier. After launch, the data confirmed the gap.

Testes em ambientes controlados nao conseguiram prever que o contexto real — tempo, lugar, privacidade, iluminacao — seria uma barreira significativa. Apos o lancamento, os dados confirmaram a lacuna.

1.2%
Completed the main flow at launch
18%
Left the camera screen immediately
22%
Exited the site after activation
Objective

Get from 1.2% to at least 5% without breaking revenue

Research and hold squad workshops to build strong hypotheses. Design simple, fast tests to increase main flow usage without compromising the revenue stream from existing e-commerce partners.

Pesquisar e realizar workshops com o squad para construir hipoteses solidas. Projetar testes simples e rapidos para aumentar o uso do fluxo principal sem comprometer a receita dos parceiros.

Process

Three phases: Empathise to Conceptualise to Design

1. Empathise 2. Conceptualise 3. Design
1. Empathise

No real user access — so we mined our own data

Without direct user access, we extracted insights from drop-off analytics via Amplitude, screen recordings, past usability tests, and competitive benchmarking against 3DLook and Wannafashion.

Sem acesso direto a usuarios, extraimos insights de analytics de abandono via Amplitude, gravacoes de tela, testes de usabilidade anteriores e benchmarking competitivo contra 3DLook e Wannafashion.

Main data insight The primary drop-off happened before users reached the main flow — the problem was upstream. O principal abandono ocorria antes dos usuarios chegarem ao fluxo principal — o problema era anterior.
Competitor insight 3DLook and Wannafashion used richer product page triggers and contextual onboarding to set expectations before the camera opened. 3DLook e Wannafashion usavam gatilhos mais ricos e onboarding contextual para preparar o usuario antes da camera abrir.
2. Conceptualise

Workshops, affinity mapping, and CAD matrix

Squad workshops produced hypotheses clustered into an Affinity Map and refined through a CAD matrix (Certainties, Assumptions, Doubts) covering every aspect of the product flow.

Workshops com o squad produziram hipoteses agrupadas em um Mapa de Afinidade e refinadas por uma matriz CAD (Certezas, Suposicoes, Duvidas) cobrindo cada aspecto do fluxo do produto.

Affinity map findings Lack of user feedback and guidance. Lack of product value communication. Identical branched flows causing cognitive confusion. Falta de feedback e orientacao ao usuario. Falta de comunicacao do valor do produto. Fluxos ramificados identicos gerando confusao cognitiva.
CAD matrix findings Users may be outside appropriate photo context. May not know they need to tap START. Onboarding lacked value delivery details. Usuarios podem estar fora do contexto adequado para fotos. Podem nao saber que precisam tocar INICIAR. Onboarding nao comunicava o valor.
3. Design

4 targeted changes as a multivariable test

Rather than a full redesign, we implemented 4 fast, isolated changes to accelerate data collection and validate hypotheses in production.

Em vez de um redesign completo, implementamos 4 mudancas rapidas e isoladas para acelerar a coleta de dados e validar hipoteses em producao.

Decision 1 - Removed non-functional onboarding The splash screen added friction without delivering value. Removed entirely from the flow. A tela de splash adicionava atrito sem entregar valor. Removida completamente do fluxo.
Decision 2 - Revised main flow invitation Redesigned the widget trigger on the product page to better communicate value and reduce confusion. O gatilho do widget na pagina de produto foi redesenhado para comunicar melhor o valor e reduzir a confusao.
Decision 3 - Enhanced camera UI activation Made the START action more prominent and explained what would happen next. A acao INICIAR foi tornada mais proeminente e explicou o que aconteceria a seguir.
Decision 4 - Improved in-flow guidance Real-time feedback during photo capture to reduce abandonment at the hardest step. Feedback em tempo real durante a captura de fotos para reduzir o abandono na etapa mais difícil.
Outcomes

200% conversion lift. 285% feature adoption growth.
